What is this App?

So basicaly this is the MRI Hotel management system built with Google Apps Script. Its used to manage room bookings, guest check-ins and checkouts, generate invoices and quotes, and track income and expenses. Think of it like a digital front desk where you can handle evrything from one place.

What Can You Do With It?

  • Room Management - Add, edit, and manage hotel rooms with diffrent types and rates
  • Bookings - Create new bookings, track guest details, and manage reservations
  • Checkout & Invoicing - Process checkouts and generate professional PDF invoices
  • Standalone Invoices - Create invoices with rooms, activities, and services with auto-numbering (INV-0001)
  • Quotes - Create quotations with rooms, activities, and services. Convert accepted quotes to invoices
  • Multi-Currency - Support for MVR and USD currencies per document
  • Tax System - Optional GST (percentage) and Green Tax (per person per night) on invoices and quotes
  • Finance Tracking - Record income and expenses with categories, currency support, and running balance
  • Budget Management - Set monthly budgets and track spending with visual progress bars
  • Categories - Manage income and expense categories for better financial tracking
  • Monthly Reports - Generate detailed financial reports with category breakdowns
  • PDF to Drive - Save invoice and quote PDFs directly to Google Drive
  • Hotel Settings - Configure hotel details, logo, default currency, and tax rates
  • Dashboard - Visual overview with charts showing room occupancy, revenue, invoices, and budget
  • User Management - Manage staff accounts with Admin and User roles
  • Export Data - Export any table to CSV, PDF, or print directly
